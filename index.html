The Reality

The Wellness Industry
Left Your Workers Behind

Every corporate wellness program on the market was designed for someone sitting at a desk in a climate-controlled office. Meditation apps. Yoga subscriptions. Therapy chatbots.

Your workers are pulling 10-hour shifts in the heat, absorbing physical and psychological stress that no app was built to address — and the industry knows it.

"We tried a wellness app last year. Nobody used it after the first week."
— HR Director, Mid-Size Contractor

Shift was built by people who've spent decades in the trades. We know what works on a jobsite — and it's not a mindfulness push notification.

20–40%+

3x

$6B+
